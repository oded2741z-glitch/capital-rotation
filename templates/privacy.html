Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

The Sectors Money Flow (the "Site") is a free tool that visualizes the relative performance of US sector ETFs. This policy explains what information is handled when you visit the Site.

1No Account, No Personal Data

The Site does not require registration or login. We do not ask for, collect, or store names, email addresses, or any personal account information. There is nothing for you to sign up for.

2Market Data

The sector performance figures shown on the Site are retrieved from Yahoo Finance by our server and displayed to all visitors. This market data is public information and is not connected to you in any way.

3Analytics & Server Logs

To understand general usage and keep the Site running, limited, non-identifying technical data may be processed:

  • Analytics: Aggregated information such as browser type, device type, and approximate region may be collected to measure traffic. This data is aggregated and not used to identify individuals.
  • Server logs: Standard request logs (such as IP address and timestamp) may be recorded by our hosting provider for security and reliability, and are not used for tracking.

4Cookies

The Site itself does not use cookies to track you. If an analytics provider is enabled, it may set its own cookies; you can control or block cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because the Site does not maintain user accounts or store personal data, there is no personal profile to access or delete. Any tracking by third-party analytics can be managed through your browser settings or the provider's own opt-out tools.

6Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

7Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@toclipskit.com.
